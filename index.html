Biography

I am a researcher at Alibaba DAMO Academy, supervised by Dr. Le Lu. Before that, I received the B.E. and M.S.E degrees in software engineering at Dalian University of Technology (DLUT), China, in 2019 and 2022. My research interests are in medical image registration, medical image analysis and machine learning.
